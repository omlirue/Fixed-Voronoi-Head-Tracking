Welcome

Thank you for taking part! You'll control a cursor with your head movements. Before the main tests there are three short calibration steps — this is normal, so here's what to expect:

  1. A head-movement calibration (follow a dot around the screen).
  2. A second calibration that tunes the system to you.
  3. A final quick steadiness check.

Then the tests begin. The whole session takes about an hour, and you'll be guided at every step.
