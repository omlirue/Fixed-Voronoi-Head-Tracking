🎯 Circle Overlap Geometry Explained

Understanding the Math Behind Target Spacing in Fitts' Law Experiments
📐 Step 1: The Basic Setup (8 Targets in a Circle)

What you're looking at:

• 8 targets arranged in a circle around a center point (home circle)

• Each target is placed at equal angular intervals: 0°, 45°, 90°, 135°, 180°, 225°, 270°, 315°

• A (Amplitude) = Distance from center to each target's center

• W (Width) = Diameter of each target circle

📏 Step 2: Angular Separation Between Adjacent Targets
Angular Separation = 360° ÷ 8 = 45°

Key Point: The angle between any two adjacent targets is always 45°

• Full circle = 360°

• Number of targets = 8

• Angle between adjacent targets = 360° ÷ 8 = 45°

📐 Step 3: Chord Length (Straight-Line Distance Between Target Centers)
1 The Problem: We need the straight-line distance between two adjacent target centers (the orange line), NOT the curved arc distance.
2 The Formula: For two points on a circle separated by angle θ, the chord length is:
Chord Length = 2 × R × sin(θ/2)
Where:
  • R = radius (in our case, Amplitude A)
  • θ = angle between points = 45°
3 Applying the Formula:
Chord Length = 2 × A × sin(45°/2)
= 2 × A × sin(22.5°)
= 2 × A × 0.3827
= 0.765 × A
🎯 Step 4: Gap Between Target Circles (The Critical Part!)

Understanding the Gap:

1 Chord Length: Distance between target centers = 0.765 × A
2 Target Radius: Each target extends W/2 from its center
3 Gap Calculation:
Gap = Chord Length - 2 × (Target Radius)
Gap = 0.765 × A - 2 × (W/2)
Gap = 0.765 × A - W
4 No Overlap Condition:
For no overlap: Gap ≥ 0
0.765 × A - W ≥ 0
0.765 × A ≥ W
A ≥ W ÷ 0.765
A ≥ 1.31 × W

Translation: The amplitude must be at least 1.31 times the target width to prevent overlap!

📊 Step 6: Your Experiment's Specific Calculations

For a 1920×1080 display:

Limiting Dimension: min(1920, 1080) = 1080px
Largest Target (17%):
W = 0.17 × 1080px = 183.6px
Minimum Amplitude Needed:
A ≥ W ÷ 0.765
A ≥ 183.6px ÷ 0.765
A ≥ 240px
A ≥ 240px ÷ 1080px = 22.2%
Your Choice:
✓ You selected 25% amplitude (270px on 1080p)
This provides a comfortable margin: 270px > 240px minimum
Safety margin: (270 - 240) ÷ 240 = 12.5% extra space

Gap Verification:

Gap = 0.765 × A - W
Gap = 0.765 × 270px - 183.6px
Gap = 206.6px - 183.6px

Result: Adjacent targets are separated by 23 pixels of clear space!
